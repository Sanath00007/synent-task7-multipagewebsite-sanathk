About Lumen Studio

A small team that designs and builds websites with care

Lumen Studio started as a one-person freelance practice and grew into a small team of designers and developers. We work with founders, small businesses, and students who need a website that looks professional and works reliably — without a long, complicated process.

Our Mission

To make good web design accessible to every small business and individual, not just large companies.

We believe a website doesn't need to be expensive or complicated to be effective. Our mission is to deliver clear, well-built websites quickly, and to explain our process honestly along the way — so clients understand exactly what they're getting and why.

What Guides Us

Our values

A few principles shape how we approach every project, big or small.

Honest communication

We explain timelines, costs, and trade-offs clearly, so there are no surprises during the project.

Thoughtful design

Every layout and color choice is made with the end user in mind, not just visual trends.

Reliable delivery

We set realistic deadlines and keep clients updated throughout, so projects stay on track.
